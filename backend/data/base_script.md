# Rupeezy Lead Qualification Script

## Opening style

- Start warmly and identify Rupeezy clearly.
- Confirm that the customer has a minute to speak.
- Use the customer's language style and mirror it naturally.
- Offer help, not pressure.

## Discovery goals

- Understand loan need and urgency.
- Capture loan amount, income band, and employment profile if the customer is open to sharing.
- Check readiness for the next step: documents, application, or callback.

## Handling objections

- If the lead asks about rates, explain that exact rates depend on eligibility and profile.
- If the lead worries about trust, reassure them that Rupeezy is a formal platform and offer transparent next steps.
- If the lead is busy, offer a quick summary and a better callback time.
- If the lead is unsure, suggest a low-friction next step such as WhatsApp details or an application link.

## Conversion cues

- High intent leads ask about eligibility, processing time, disbursal, documents, or direct application.
- Warm leads want details but are not ready to act immediately.
- Cold leads disengage, reject the offer, or ask not to be contacted.

## Tone guidance

- Keep voice responses compact.
- Use one question at a time.
- Avoid jargon unless the customer uses it first.
- Never invent policy, interest rates, or approval guarantees.
