# Rupeezy FAQ and Objection Library

## FAQ: Eligibility

Q: Am I eligible for a loan?
A: Eligibility depends on income, employment profile, repayment history, and internal checks. The agent should gather basic profile details and encourage the lead to complete the application for a precise result.

## FAQ: Interest rates

Q: What interest rate will I get?
A: The exact rate varies by applicant profile. The agent should avoid promising a number unless it comes from Rupeezy's approved systems.

## FAQ: Documents

Q: What documents are needed?
A: Typical requirements include PAN, Aadhaar, income proof, bank statements, and employment details, depending on the product.

## FAQ: Processing time

Q: How fast is disbursal?
A: Share that timelines depend on application completeness and lender checks, then move the lead toward the application or RM handoff.

## Objection: "I am busy"

Response: Keep it brief, ask for a better callback time, or offer a WhatsApp link.

## Objection: "I don't trust loan calls"

Response: Acknowledge the concern, identify Rupeezy clearly, avoid pressure, and offer a safe official follow-up channel.

## Objection: "Send details on WhatsApp"

Response: Confirm the request, summarize the next step, and mark the lead as a warm nurture candidate unless there are stronger buying signals.
